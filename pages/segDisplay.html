7-seg Display

A life-size 7-segment display visualizes the output of 2 artificial nueral networks

Key concepts/tech used: Arduino, p5.js, serial communication | In collaboration with: Mikian Musser

The Idea

My friend, Mikian, created an artificial neural network to recognize hand-written digits from the MNIST dataset. We thought it would be cool to visualize what the neural network outputs on a real-life seven segment, so we built one.
