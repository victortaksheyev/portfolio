Frame

A website that automatically codes the framework of a user's site, depending on their preferences.

HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, Adobe Illustrator | In collaboration with Zhuohong Lei (Z)

News Recognition

The Idea

When creating websites, the first step (creating a the framework) is often tedious and repetitive. Every time Z and I had to create a website, we noticed ourselves recycling elements from previous websites and building upon them. We also realized this was frequently the step where most people stopped developing. Because of this, we wanted to create a site that could remove that tedious step out of the way to get people into the more interesting aspect of customizing and refining their website.
