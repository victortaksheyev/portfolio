Join SEDS UNLV


Students for the Exploration and Development of Space (SEDS) empowers students to participate and make an impact in space exploration. SEDS helps students develop their technical and leadership skills by providing opportunities to manage and participate in national projects as well as to attend conferences, publish their work, and develop their professional network, in order to help students become more effective in their present and future careers in industry, academia, government, and education.


Experience

As a member of SEDS, you will be immersed in the design and development of rockets, payloads, and other space systems.

Networking

There are many networking opportunities for SEDS members to meet top aerospace companies like NASA, Lockheed Martin, and ULA.
